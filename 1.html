1.Creaks

: Creaks is a platform adventure game developed by Amanita Design.
It is the first major game by Amanita Design that is not a point & click adventure.

Creaks begins in the bedroom of the unnamed player character, who is seen sitting at his desk
when the light flickers and dies, and the wallpaper peels away to reveal a secret tunnel behind his wall.
